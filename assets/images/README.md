# Images Directory

## Recommended Images for Your IoT Website

### Hero Banner Background (Optional)
- **hero-bg.jpg** - 1920x1080px or higher
  - Suggested: Smart home interior, IoT devices, modern technology
  - Will overlay with particle effects and neon patterns

### About Section
- **about-visual.jpg** - 800x600px
  - Company facility, team photo, or IoT device showcase

### Product Images
- **product-1.jpg** through **product-n.jpg**
  - 800x800px square format
  - Smart lighting, security systems, climate control, energy management devices

### Service Images
- **service-1.jpg** through **service-n.jpg**
  - 800x600px landscape format
  - Installation, maintenance, consulting services

### Icons & Logos
- **logo.png** - Your company logo (transparent background)
- **favicon.ico** - 32x32px or 16x16px

## Current Visual Effects
The site currently uses:
- Animated gradient backgrounds
- Particle.js network animation
- Neon line effects
- SVG icon graphics
- CSS animations and transitions

All visual effects are implemented and working without requiring image files.
